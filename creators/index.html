Facebook is for real community that goes a long way

Every month, more than 3 billion people around the world open Facebook to connect with the people and topics that they care about. That kind of reach gives creators a truly global stage, whether you're just starting out or looking to grow the community that you already have.

But what makes Facebook different isn't just the numbers – it's the people. The conversations here are two-way. Your community doesn't just follow you – they root for you, share your work and help you keep going. On Facebook, it's not about building alone. It's about creating something meaningful with the support of people who care about what you do.

And when you're ready to grow, Facebook gives you the tools to do it. From building community to making content creation a career, creators have real opportunities to turn their passions into income. In fact, Facebook paid creators over USD 2 billion last year – and we're not slowing down.
